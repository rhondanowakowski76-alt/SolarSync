Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Solar Sync (ABN 57 363 510 051), trading as SolarSync CRM ("we", "us", "SolarSync CRM"), operates the SolarSync CRM platform at solarsynccrm.com.au. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. What we collect

  • Account data: name, business name, ABN, email, phone, and login credentials (PINs are encrypted; we use authenticator-app two-factor — we do not store passwords).
  • Customer & job data entered by our business users: client names, addresses, contact details, site/installation details, quotes, photos and compliance records.
  • Payment data: processed securely by Stripe — we do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage data: log data, device/browser information and actions within the platform.

2. How we use it

To provide and operate the platform, process payments, generate quotes and compliance documents, provide support, send service communications, and meet legal obligations.

3. Storage & security

Data is hosted on secure servers located in Australia (Sydney). We use encryption in transit, encrypted secrets, two-factor authentication and access controls. No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

4. Disclosure

We do not sell your data. We share it only with service providers who help us operate (e.g. Stripe for payments, our cloud host), where required by law, or with your consent. Each business using SolarSync CRM is responsible for the customer data it enters.

5. Access & correction

You may request access to, or correction of, your personal information by contacting us at hello@solarsynccrm.com.au.

6. Complaints

If you have a privacy concern, contact us first at hello@solarsynccrm.com.au. If unresolved, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies / local storage to keep you signed in and run the app.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; the latest version is always available at solarsynccrm.com.au/privacy.
